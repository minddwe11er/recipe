Main Courses

Hungarian Bograch

Bogrács is a traditional Hungarian stew cooked over an open fire in a large kettle. It’s made with tender beef, paprika, potatoes, and sometimes vegetables, creating a bold, smoky, and slightly spicy flavor that reflects its rustic origins.

Ukrainian Borscht

A hearty and colorful beetroot soup from Eastern Europe, borscht is packed with vegetables, often includes meat, and is traditionally served with sour cream. It’s rich in flavor and loved for its comforting warmth and vibrant red color.

Pasta Balognese

This classic Italian pasta dish features a rich meat-based sauce simmered with tomatoes, onions, and herbs. Spaghetti Bolognese is beloved for its comforting flavors and is a favorite family meal all around the world.

Carbonara

A creamy Roman pasta made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper — no cream involved! Carbonara is a quick and delicious dish with deep, savory flavors and a silky texture.
