Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients: 200g spaghetti 100g pancetta or bacon 2 eggs 50g grated Parmesan Pepper

Instructions: Cook spaghetti. In a pan, fry pancetta. In a bowl, whisk eggs with cheese and pepper. Combine hot spaghetti with pancetta and remove from heat. Quickly mix in egg mixture to form a creamy sauce.
