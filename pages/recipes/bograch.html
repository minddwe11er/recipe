Hungarian Bograch

Ingredients: 500g beef or pork 2 onions 2 carrots 2 potatoes Paprika, garlic, bay leaf Salt, pepper 1 liter broth

Instructions: Sear meat in a large pot. Add chopped onions, garlic, and paprika. Add carrots, potatoes, bay leaf, and broth. Simmer until meat and vegetables are soft. Adjust seasoning to taste.
