Borscht (Ukrainian Beet Soup)

Ingredients: 2 beets 2 potatoes 1 carrot 1 onion 1/4 cabbage head 2 tbsp tomato paste Vegetable or meat broth Salt, pepper Sour cream for serving

Instructions: Peel and chop all vegetables. In a large pot, saut√© onion and carrot, add beets, tomato paste, and broth. Simmer for 20 minutes, then add potatoes and cabbage. Cook until tender. Season and serve with sour cream.
