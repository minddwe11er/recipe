Appetizers

Bruschetta with Tomato and Basil

Ingredients: 1 baguette 3 ripe tomatoes 2 cloves garlic Fresh basil leaves Olive oil Salt and pepper
Instructions: Slice the baguette and toast it lightly. Dice the tomatoes and mix with chopped basil, salt, pepper, and olive oil. Rub toasted bread with garlic and top with the tomato mixture. Serve immediately.

Stuffed Mushrooms

Ingredients: 12 button mushrooms 100g cream cheese 1 clove garlic (minced) Parsley (chopped) Salt and pepper
Instructions: Remove stems from mushrooms and mix them with cream cheese, garlic, parsley, salt, and pepper. Fill mushroom caps with the mixture. Bake at 180Â°C for 15-20 minutes.

Deviled Eggs

Ingredients: 6 hard-boiled eggs 2 tbsp mayonnaise 1 tsp mustard Salt and pepper Paprika for garnish
Instructions: Halve the eggs and remove yolks. Mash yolks with mayonnaise, mustard, salt, and pepper. Spoon or pipe the mixture back into the egg whites. Sprinkle with paprika.
