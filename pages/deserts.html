Deserts

Chocolate Mousse

Ingredients: 200g dark chocolate 3 eggs, separated 2 tbsp sugar 1/2 cup heavy cream
Instructions: Melt chocolate and cool slightly. Beat egg yolks with sugar, mix in chocolate. Whip cream, beat egg whites separately, and fold both into chocolate mixture. Chill for at least 2 hours.

Apple Crumble

Ingredients: 4 apples 100g flour 50g sugar 50g butter Cinnamon
Instructions: Peel and slice apples, place in a baking dish. Mix flour, sugar, butter, and cinnamon to make crumbs. Sprinkle over apples and bake at 180Â°C for 30-40 minutes.

Tiramisu (Quick Version)

Ingredients: 250g mascarpone 100ml coffee 3 tbsp sugar Ladyfingers Cocoa powder
Instructions: Dip ladyfingers in coffee and layer in a dish. Mix mascarpone with sugar and spread over. Add another layer. Dust with cocoa powder and chill.
